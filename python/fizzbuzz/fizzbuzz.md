Fizz Buzz is a classic programming interview exercise designed to test basic coding ability, such as loops and conditional logic. The task is to print numbers from 1 to 100, replacing multiples of 3 with "Fizz", multiples of 5 with "Buzz", and multiples of both 3 and 5 with "FizzBuzz"

Muliples of 5 ( 5, 10, 15, 20, 25) - Buzz
Multiple of 5 and 3 (15, 30, 45, 60, 75) - FizzBuzz
